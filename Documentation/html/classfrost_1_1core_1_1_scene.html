Detailed Description

In the engine, your game is divided into scenes, and the engine renders the game in the scene that is currently defined as active.

Each scene has its own GameObject Tree<T>, on which you can act by adding/removing entities, children of existing GameObjects or by adding/removing components associated with the scene's GameObjects.
